Indicator 5 Under Construction

Custodial Concentration

Concern

Is custody concentrating into a small number of intermediaries that could be coerced or compromised?

~30%

Supply held by top-10 custodians (ETFs, exchanges, treasuries)

Custodial share has roughly doubled since the January 2024 launch of US spot Bitcoin ETFs, driven by ETF inflows, corporate treasury accumulation (notably Strategy), and persistent exchange balances. Self-custody as a share of circulating supply is at multi-year lows. Concentrated custody re-introduces the trusted-third-party risk Bitcoin was designed to remove: holdings can be frozen, seized, or rehypothecated by intermediaries.
